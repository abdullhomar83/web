Welcome to Qaddim Gate

Your Comprehensive Research Submission System

Qaddim Gate is an online submission system, a comprehensive and user-friendly platform designed to facilitate the efficient management of research abstracts within King Saud bin Abdul Aziz University for Health Sciences.

This system serves as a central hub for the submission, review, and administration of research abstracts, streamlining the entire process for conference organizers, reviewers, and researchers alike.
